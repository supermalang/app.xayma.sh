# MARKETING STRATEGY & PLAN — Xayma.sh
> v2 · Last updated: March 2026

---

## 1. Positioning

**Target market:** West Africa (Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Mali, Cameroon — primarily Francophone)
**Primary buyer:** SME owner or IT integrator who needs web applications but can't afford to manage infrastructure
**Core positioning statement:**

> *"The simplest way to get web applications running for your business in West Africa — pay as you go, in FCFA, with Wave or Orange Money."*

**Emotional hook:** You shouldn't need a server admin to run your business software. Xayma.sh handles everything so you can focus on your customers.

## 4. Sales Process

### Direct Customers (Inbound/Outreach)
```
Awareness → Landing page or WhatsApp outreach
Interest → Watch 2-min demo video on homepage
Consideration → Free 7-day trial (1 Starter instance, no credit required)
Decision → Top up credits → deploy in under 5 minutes
Retention → WhatsApp credit alerts, quick top-up via Wave
```

### Resellers (Outbound-first)
```
Identification → LinkedIn search for "intégrateur web applications Sénégal / Côte d'Ivoire"
Outreach → Personalized LinkedIn message or WhatsApp intro (via sales consultant)
Demo → 30-min screenshare: deploy an instance in real time, show reseller dashboard
Trial → Onboard with Small Bundle (10 instances, 30 days)
Expansion → Upgrade to Medium/Large bundle within 60 days
```

## 5. Pricing Communication

**FCFA-first messaging:** Never show EUR or USD as primary price to West African audience.

| Plan | Headline price | Message |
|------|---------------|---------|
| Starter | 10,000 FCFA / mois | "Pour les petites équipes qui démarrent" |
| Pro | 20,000 FCFA / mois | "Pour une PME qui grandit" |
| Enterprise | 50,000 FCFA / mois | "Pour les équipes exigeantes" |
| Reseller Small | à partir de 8,000 FCFA / instance | "Jusqu'à 20% de remise. Revendez plus, gagnez plus." |

**Trust signals to highlight:**
- ✅ Paiement Wave & Orange Money
- ✅ Prix en FCFA, facturation locale
- ✅ Données hébergées en Europe (RGPD)
- ✅ Support en français
- ✅ Pas de contrat, résiliez quand vous voulez
